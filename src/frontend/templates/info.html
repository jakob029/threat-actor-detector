Introduction

This is an LLM-powered app designed for identifying and attributing threat actors based on discovered Indicators of Compromise (IoCs).

Features and Usage

    IoC Analysis: Analyze indicators like IP addresses, domains, file hashes, and other IoCs to identify potential threat actors.

    Conversation: .

    History: Review previously identified threats.

    Statistics: Visual summaries of threats and methods used.

How to Use the Application

    1. Enter TTPs and potential IoCs for a given attack.

    2. The system analyzes the IoCs and compares them with known patterns.

    3. Results are presented as a report, detailing possible threat actors, their methods, graphs and related APT:s.
